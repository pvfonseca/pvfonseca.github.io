Paulo da Fonseca

Lecturer in Economics

Santa Catarina State University

I am a Lecturer in Economics at Santa Catarina State University, Brazil. My research interests are in Macroeconomics; Fiscal policy and monetary policy and its interactions; Dynamic stochastic general equilibrium (DSGE) models; Heterogeneous agents New Keynesian models; Agent-based modeling and Computational methods.
